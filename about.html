About Me

This is what I do.

Sanity and insanity are words we made up -- and maybe we need to change their definition. I recently discovered that I want, with my life, to help erase the stigma associated with mental illness. Mankind must have compassion for all life and respect that people's problems don't always make logical sense. Mankind must undersatnd that it simply CAN'T make sense of everything -- especially other people. We're surrounded by mysteries, and we must embrace that, not shove it away. Embrace the ignorance, embrace the lack of responsibility, lack of guilt! Embrace it until you're taught the next lesson, and then learn, and then enjoy. And then learn, and then enjoy. But try, against all odds, to stay unstuck. Help others stay unstuck. Love all ♥

I don't really know what will help the world the most. Right now, I think I can change the world for the best by helping the mentally ill. It is the struggle I am most personally connected to, and with its elimination, we will have so many more people to solve all the rest of the problems of the world. People are wasting away their lives, not knowing their full potential. Isn't that the full potential of the world -- when each of its individual parts reach it too? I want every human being I can reach to reach the stars, whatever that means to them.
